**Method**
  1. Estimate gamma for 2006-2025 
  2. Estimate gamma for each individual season 
  3. Compare individual gammas to the accepted value of gamma (1.82)
   
**Results**
- Gamma estimate for 2006-2025: 1.77635
  - 95% profile confidence interval: (1.722653, 1.830214)
    - Agrees with the accepted value
- The accepted value of 1.82 falls inside the CIs for each season except 2010
  - This is what Dayaratna and Miller found, albeit for their first-order model
